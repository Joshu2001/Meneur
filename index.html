Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

1. Our Core Principle

Meneur is designed as a "local-only" productivity tool. This means that all data you input into the app—including tasks, schedules, notes, and preferences—is stored exclusively on your device.

We do not have access to your data, we do not store it on our servers, and we do not sell it to third parties.

2. Information Collection

  • User-Generated Content: All tasks and lists stay on your device.
  • Personal Information: The App does not require an account, email, or name.
  • Usage Data: We do not track behavior or use off-device analytics.

3. Data Storage & Security

Because data is stored locally, its security depends on your device. We recommend using built-in security features like passcodes or biometrics.

No Cloud Sync: Meneur does not sync with cloud services by default. External backups (iCloud/Google Drive) are governed by your provider settings.

4. Third-Party Services

No links to external data collectors or third-party ads exist within the app.

5. Children's Privacy

We collect zero personal information from any user, including children under 13.
